# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Citizens seeking government services and reporting issues. They use the platform to navigate public services, file complaints, and discover schemes relevant to them.

## Product Purpose
To make government services accessible, efficient, and proactive through AI. Success looks like a citizen seamlessly reporting an issue or finding a scheme without getting lost in bureaucratic interfaces.

## Brand Personality
Premium, futuristic, polished. The platform should evoke confidence and delight, presenting government services with the same fidelity as top-tier consumer tech.

## Anti-references
Generic SaaS dashboards. It should not look like another generic B2B tool, nor should it look like a cluttered legacy government portal.

## Design Principles
- **Elevated Functionality:** Make utilitarian actions feel extraordinary through micro-interactions and premium depth.
- **Clarity Over Clutter:** Guide the user with clear hierarchy; don't overwhelm them with data tables unless necessary.
- **AI as an Enabler:** Ensure AI features (like the chat assistant or predictive search) feel integrated and magical, not bolted on.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA compliance (high contrast, keyboard navigable), adhering to standard best practices for inclusive digital public goods.
